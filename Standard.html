Who is Panda Eyes?

Oskar Steinbeck, otherwise known by his stagename Panda Eyes, is an artist that mainly specializes in Electronic Dance Music. His music is usually featured in Electronic Dance Music Promotion Channels such as: DubstepGutter, Diversity, Xkito and most recently UKF Dubstep. He started making music at the age of 13 and has been making music ever since. The genre's of music he tends to specialize in are, Dubstep, Glitch-Hop, Electro-House, and he also lies to experiment with some other styles of music as well. Like his latest album "Continue", he expandeed his sound and did some other interesting things, like Funk and Jazz.

A couple of days before he released his album "Continue", he posted a video on his Youtube channel talking about his life. He goes into detail about how he started making music, his life in school, and the disease that he was diagnosed with. It's a pretty inspirational video if you ask me, it shows how far he's come and the struggles he went through to be where he is today.
